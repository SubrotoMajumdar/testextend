Epidemiologic Studies in Hyponatremia

  • Shapiro DS, Sonnenblick M, Galperin I, Melkonyan L, Munter G. Severe hyponatraemia in elderly hospitalized patients: prevalence, aetiology and outcome. Intern Med J. 2010;40(8):574-580.

About Tomas Berl, MD
Tomas Berl, MD, is an internationally renowned nephrologist
Tomas Berl, MD, is Professor of Medicine in the Division of Renal Diseases and Hypertension at the University of Colorado. He was President of the American Society of Nephrology (ASN) in 2005 and is a member of several other professional organizations, including the American Society of Clinical Investigation (ASCI) and the American Association of Physicians (AAP). Professor Berl has authored or co-authored more than 215 peer-reviewed scientific articles, editorials, reviews, and book chapters.
Disclosure: Dr. Berl is a paid consultant for Otsuka America Pharmaceutical, Inc. He has also consulted for Astellas Pharma, Inc., and is on advisory boards of Bristol-Myers Squibb and AstraZeneca LP. Dr. Berl is chair of the Data Safety Monitoring Committee for the Nephron-D trial run by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA).
