About Amir K. Jaffer, MD
Amir K. Jaffer, MD, FHM, is associate professor of medicine and chief of the Division of Hospital Medicine at the University of Miami. He was previously the medical director of the Internal Medicine Preoperative Assessment, Consultation and Treatment (IMPACT) Center and the Anticoagulation Clinic at the Cleveland Clinic Foundation in Cleveland, Ohio. Dr. Jaffer is an associate editor for the Cleveland Clinic Journal of Medicine, an editorial board member of the British Journal of Medical Practitioners, and a governing board member of the Society for Perioperative Assessment and Quality Improvement.
Disclosure: Dr. Jaffer is a paid consultant for Otsuka America Pharmaceutical, Inc. He has also consulted for Sanofi-Aventis, AstraZeneca, Boehringer Ingelheim, and Canyon Pharmaceuticals.
